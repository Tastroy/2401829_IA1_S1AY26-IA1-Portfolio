Chosen Idea: 4.AI Based Adaptive Learning Systems.

An AI powered tool that makes learning easier, efficient and more engaging for students.

How to apply this in the world.

This can be applied in the world by developing an AI tool that can help students learn programming by providing interactive lessons, personalized feedback and real-time assistance.

Why is this Useful?

This is useful because it can help students learn programming more effectively by providing personalized learning experiences that cater to their individual needs and learning styles.

The Ethical Issues with this type of AI-based Tool.

  • Student Data Privacy
  • Over-reliance on AI

How to Mitigate These Issues?

  • Implement robust data security measures to protect student information and ensure data protection regulations.
  • Encourage a balanced approach to learning by promoting the use of AI tools as supplements to traditional teaching methods rather than replacements.

Example of AI Based Adaptive Learning App

Duolingo- Doulingo is a popular language learning app that uses AI to provide personalized lessons and feedback to users.
